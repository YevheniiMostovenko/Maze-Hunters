ABOUT US

Heyo! We are a beginner band Maze Hunters, consisting of three musicians: Dimitar - bass, Nikita - drums, Alexander - vocals / guitar. We have been born in different countries - Moldova and Ukraine, but the capital of Bulgaria has gathered us to play together and slowly move on to our dreams. We would be glad to introduce our indie-rock / alternative rock music, having the task of doing what we love and to be liked by you. We are aware that we are yet to enter the world of music and we still have a lot to learn, but we believe that step by step we will be able to build a whole that will bring satisfaction and pleasant emotions.
